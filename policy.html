Policies & Declaration of Work

This document applies to all services provided by BusinessPal across Northern Ireland and the United Kingdom.

1. Business Overview

BusinessPal is a web design and website development service based in Northern Ireland, providing digital services to businesses across the UK. Services may include website design, development, subscriptions, and ongoing maintenance.

2. Scope of Work

All services are provided based on an agreed scope confirmed in writing prior to commencement. This may include, but is not limited to:

  • Custom or WordPress website development
  • Website redesigns or improvements
  • Subscription-based website services
  • Ongoing maintenance and content updates
  • Basic SEO and performance configuration

3. Free Mockups & Previews

BusinessPal may provide free mockups or previews prior to project approval. These materials remain the intellectual property of BusinessPal and may not be reused, distributed, or developed without written permission unless the project proceeds.

4. Subscription Services

Subscription-based services are billed monthly and may include development, updates, and support as agreed. BusinessPal reserves the right to amend or terminate subscriptions with reasonable notice where necessary.

5. Maintenance & Support

Maintenance services cover reasonable updates and fixes related to the original build. Major changes, new features, or third-party issues fall outside standard maintenance and may require a separate agreement.

6. Client Responsibilities

Clients are responsible for supplying accurate content, ensuring ownership or licensing of all provided materials, and reviewing work in a timely manner. Delays caused by missing content or feedback may affect timelines.

7. Payments & Fees

All pricing is agreed in advance. Payments must be made within agreed terms. Late or missed payments may result in suspension of services. All prices are in GBP (£) unless stated otherwise.

8. Intellectual Property

Upon full payment, clients receive rights to the final delivered website. BusinessPal retains ownership of internal tools, reusable code, and the right to display completed work in portfolios and promotional material.

9. Data Protection & GDPR

BusinessPal processes personal data in accordance with UK GDPR principles. Clients remain responsible for ensuring their website content, privacy notices, and data handling practices are compliant with applicable regulations.

10. Liability & Limitations

BusinessPal provides services in good faith and to a professional standard. No guarantees are made regarding business performance, search rankings, revenue, or uninterrupted service. Liability is limited to the value of services provided.

11. Declaration of Work

By engaging BusinessPal, the client confirms they have read, understood, and accepted these policies. This document constitutes a professional declaration governing the working relationship.

12. Game-Based Promotions & Reward Verification

From time to time, BusinessPal may offer promotional incentives or rewards linked to interactive content or games hosted on the BusinessPal website. These promotions are provided for entertainment and demonstration purposes only.

Any claim for a reward, including but not limited to a free website build, must be verified before approval. Verification requires the submission of a clear and continuous screen recording showing:

  • The start of the game session
  • Uninterrupted gameplay
  • The final qualifying score clearly visible

Edited, shortened, accelerated, or manipulated recordings will not be accepted. BusinessPal reserves the right to request additional verification where necessary or to reject any submission deemed unfair, automated, or invalid.

Rewards are limited to one per individual or business and are subject to availability, suitability of project scope, and BusinessPal’s discretion. BusinessPal reserves the right to amend, suspend, or withdraw game-based promotions at any time without prior notice.

Participation in any game-based promotion does not create a contractual obligation or guarantee of service.
