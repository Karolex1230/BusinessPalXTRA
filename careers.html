Careers at Business Pal

We are building a modern digital business focused on helping companies grow online. While we are currently a small operation, we have ambitious goals for the future.

Our Current Stage

Business Pal is currently run as a solo venture, focused on building high-quality digital solutions for businesses and developing a strong foundation for long-term growth. At this stage we are not actively hiring new team members. However, we are continuously expanding our services and client base, and we hope to grow into a larger team within the next year. Our long-term vision is to build a talented group of professionals who share a passion for innovation, technology, and helping businesses succeed online.

Future Opportunities

As Business Pal continues to grow, we expect to open positions in areas such as:

Web Developer

Building modern websites and digital platforms.

UI/UX Designer

Designing clean, intuitive user experiences.

Digital Marketing Specialist

Helping businesses grow through online marketing.

Project Manager

Managing client projects and coordinating teams.
