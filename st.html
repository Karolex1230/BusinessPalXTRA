The Beginning

ST Auto Styling was founded by Seweryn Treder with a vision to provide high-quality vehicle styling and customisation services. When Seweryn first approached us, the business was in its early startup stage and needed a professional online presence to support its growth.

Our goal was to design and build a modern website that showcased the quality of Seweryn’s work while allowing potential customers to easily explore services, view previous projects, and get in touch.

Growth & Success

Since launching the website, ST Auto Styling has continued to grow within the automotive styling industry. The business has developed a strong reputation for quality workmanship, attention to detail, and excellent customer service.

What began as a small startup has evolved into a growing and expanding business, attracting new customers and continually developing its services.

Today, ST Auto Styling continues to build its brand and reputation, delivering high-quality vehicle styling solutions while expanding its customer base and industry presence.
